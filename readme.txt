This is a demonstration of VBLFI method, which is a blind light field image quality assessment method . The method is described in:
J. Xiang, M. Yu, H. Chen, H. Xu, Y. Song and G. Jiang, "VBLFI: VISUALIZATION-BASED BLIND LIGHT FIELD IMAGE QUALITY ASSESSMENT". in ICME , 2020.
